While many know me to undertake leadership and one who executes projects, my greatest joy comes from introspection and creative ideation. I enjoy projects that move our humanity forward and that spark leadership in others.

Over the last 12 years, I have compiled an expertise that spans architecture and graphic design to sound design and electronic music production. This background has shaped my approach to web development. I have a passion for learning new skills that I can integrate into the next product.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, you might find me backcountry camping by an alpine lake, or growing some organic vegetables in my garden.

VoterGab

Provides the voting public a tool to get informed and share endorsements of ballot measures. Users can create groups, and add members to their group, and then collectively vote on endorsements. Data is pulled from public APIs for ballot information. Each group has its own live chat, where users can weigh in on the endorsement vote. (MEAN) Live Website
