Coding Projects

LeverMap

A full suite of tools for political activists to organize behind state-level legislation. Public facing site allows visitors to observe tracked bills and campaign finance information. Combines and matches data from multiple API's. Uses Firebase database and authorization security rules. Implements technologies as AWS Lambda, API Gateway, React-Router-Dom, Re-base and React-Modal. (React/Redux/Firebase) Live Website

VoterGab

Provides the voting public a tool to get informed and share endorsements of ballot measures. Users can create groups, and add members to their group, and then collectively vote on endorsements. Data is pulled from public APIs for ballot information. Each group has its own live chat, where users can weigh in on the endorsement vote. (MEAN) Live Website | On Github

SynthGiro

A mobile synthesizer app built for iPhone with polyphonic capability. Built in Swift 3.0, it utilizes the AudioKit framework. It uses Core Motion input for tracking physical gestures and translates into sonic modulation of a low-pass filter and a delay effect. A user can plug in a midi keyboard to trigger notes as well as use the on-screen keyboard. The app includes an amplitude envelope control, a waveform slider, which phases between four waveforms, and an adjuster to affect the rate of the delay effect. (Swift/AudioKit) On Github

TravelBuddy

Built in Django framework, this provides a tool for sharing your trip plans with other users. You can post your own trips, or you can join other users' trips. A detail page shows you all the information about the trip including who has joined the trip, and who is organizing the trip. (Python/Django) On Github

Media Production

Music Composition

Since my early years in high school tinkering with a four track Tascam recorder and with electronic music equipment, music production has always been an integral part of my creative existence. I often have an approach of reinvention, taking found sounds and giving them a new quality. My goal is to elicit a nostalgic response that simultaneously feels uncharted. Soundcloud

Sound Design

I am a sound tinkerer. I deconstruct the evolution of a sound before it arrived at the listener's ear, and I'm acutely aware of timbre and spatial aspects. I notice the many layers of sounds that travel around us and bounce off surfaces and resonate with objects. I see visual media production as an extension of that awareness, and a process to create a hyper-real experience that sometimes stretches the rules of physical nature. Sound Design Page

Graphic Design

With an eye for detail, I distill a concept down to a particular message and identify a clean and simple way to convey that message graphically. Much of my graphics experience comes from designing materials for political campaigns and architectural drawings.
